\# Governance Model



Wynergy Fibre Solutions Ltd maintains ownership and direction of all repositories.



\## Roles

\*\*Maintainer\*\*  

Responsible for accepting or rejecting changes, ensuring architecture standards, and enforcing compliance.



\*\*Contributor\*\*  

Provides improvements through structured PRs.



\## Decision Process

\- Architectural changes require maintainer approval  

\- Security policies cannot be modified without review  

\- Documentation updates required for all functional changes  



\## Compliance

All repositories must comply with internal integrity, licensing, telemetry, and audit frameworks.
